Kyle Yasumiishi, CPA

Software Developer | Tax Accountant | Blockchain Enthusiast

My Story

I'm driven by my passions and love of learning.

  • Journalism

    Editor-in-Chief of The Independent

    I've always believed clear, concise writing would serve me well in life and help me serve others. In 2009, I had the privilege to lead and learn from an incredible team of student journalists. Together, we learned how to research and analyze complex topics, ethically report stories to our community, and push our campus newspaper into the digital era, winning a few statewide and national awards in the process. Read some of my published articles here!

  • Accounting

    Tax Associate at KPMG

    I studied accounting in college to learn the universal language of business. Through audit and tax internships, volunteering as a tax preparer, and attending graduate school, I also got exposed to my first million-plus line code base, the Internal Revenue Code. Spending several years studying tax laws prepared me surprisingly well for learning how to program. I started my career in public accounting, where I had the amazing opportunity to serve world-class companies in a wide variety of industries such as technology, telecommunications, retail, and real estate.

  • Software Development

    Pursuing my passion for technology

    I've always been interested in technology, and my professional experiences serving clients in the technology industry inspired me to learn programming. I discovered I have a passion for leveraging technology to solve business problems. I'm currently having the time of my life pursuing a career in software development!

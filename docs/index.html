My Story

I'm driven by my passions and love of learning.

  • Journalism

    Editor-in-Chief of The Independent

    I've always believed clear, concise writing would serve me well in life and help me serve others. In 2009, I had the privilege to lead and learn from an incredible team of student journalists. Together, we learned how to research and analyze complex issues, ethically report stories to our community, and push our campus newspaper into the digital era, winning a few statewide and national awards in the process. Read some of my published articles here!

  • Accounting

    Tax Associate at KPMG

    I studied accounting in college to learn the universal language of business. Through audit and tax internships, volunteering as a tax preparer, and attending graduate school, I also got exposed to my first million-plus line code base, the Internal Revenue Code. Learning how to analyze tax laws helped me develop the mindset and skillset of a programmer, including logical thinking, comprehending complex documentation, and, most importantly, perseverance. I started my career in public accounting, where I had the amazing opportunity to serve world-class companies in a wide variety of industries such as technology, telecommunications, retail, and real estate.

  • Software Development

    Pursuing my passion for tech

    I've always been interested in technology, and my professional experiences serving clients in the technology industry inspired me to learn programming. I discovered I have a passion for leveraging technology to solve business problems. I'm currently having the time of my life pursuing a career in software development!
